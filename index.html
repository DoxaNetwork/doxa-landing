What is this?

A cooperative publishing platform run by paid curators who are elected by community members. Our content curation network utilizes tokens

Why?

A cooperative publishing platform run by paid curators who are elected by community members. Our content curation network utilizes tokens for community membership, delegated token-weighted voting for decision making, and token payment to reward high-quality work. On this platform users will be able to create independent communities and tune the incentive and voting structures to encourage particular types of content.
